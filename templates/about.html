About Us

The idea behind the school

We begin with three core premises:

  • Learning is hard
  • Learning alone is really hard
  • Some skills are not taught in school

We believe that these skills are essential. Hence the goal of hackerschool is to make learning these skills easier by getting people past the initial hurdle through workshops and classes.

Our Goal

NUS Hackers has conducted workshops in the past. What makes this different, is its scale.

Though we are starting small, with only a few courses, we have a big ambition. As we get better and fine tune existing workshops, we'll continue to grow and add to the number of courses. The hope is to ultimately have a comprehensive list of courses that incoming freshman and all nus students can look forward to and take part in.

These courses will be continuously reviewed and conducted on a regular (yearly) basis. Such is the scale that hackerschool hopes to reach.

Our Workshops

We charge a nominal fee for our workshops. This, in our experience, translates into a significant difference in terms of the crowd we attract as well as the general atmosphere during the workshops.

The team
